Research Lab

The Research Lab is a place where people can learn and use new technologies and equipment, they may otherwise not have access to. They can get help with a project they are currently working on or they can start a new project.

Aero Lab

To be a leading Aviation Engineering Technology program to provide a skilled and apt human resource for the aviation industry. we are committed to producing proficient engineering technologists who will contribute to societal and socioeconomic needs through compliance, research, innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurship.

Pathology Lab 1

Pathology Lab 1 is a mega unit of Azra Naheed Medical College that includes four major laboratories (including Histopathology, Microbiology, Haematology, and Chemical Pathology). This lab is equipped with high research equipment that facilities the students of under graduation, master, and post-graduation in their respective research topics. This lab also provides teaching, practical demonstration, and hands-on training practice to their valuable students (including MBBS, BDS, AHS, DPT, Biological Sciences, and Post-Graduation).

Pathology Lab 2

Pathology Lab 2 is a mega unit of Azra Naheed Medical College that includes four major laboratories (includes Histopathology, Microbiology, Hematology and Chemical Pathology). This lab is equipped with highly research equipments that facilities the students of under graduation, master, and post-graduation in their respective research topics. This lab also providing teaching, practical demonstration and hands on training practice to their valuable students (includes MBBS, BDS, AHS, DPT, Biological Sciences and Post-Graduation).

Biochemistry Lab

Biochemistry Lab brings together biology & chemistry by using chemical knowledge and techniques. The Biochemistry Lab is equipped with the latest equipment that satisfies the needs of both undergraduate and post-graduation students. Access to the latest equipment and instruments reagents enables students to test and understand the concentration of biochemical parameters from various biological specimens using clinical knowledge and techniques taught by highly qualified staff during the demonstration. Azra Naheed medical college lab is able to provide the basis for practical advances in medicine, molecular biology, and biotechnology.

Anatomy Museum

Azra Naheed Medical College (ANMC), Anatomy Museum is a state-of-the-art Museum. Where we have modelled all body, original body bones, and specimens of different body organs. In which we demonstrate to the students on models, bones, and specimens for a complete understanding of theoretical and practical. We also provide bones to the student’s self-study and exam preparation.

High-Tech Research Lab

Azra Naheed Medical College (A Project of Superior University) is a mega project and has state-of-the-art laboratories that facilitate the students of medical studies (including MBBS, BDS, AHS, DPT, Biological Sciences, and Post-Graduation students) with highly equipped research equipment that helps students to proceed their research work under the supervision of highly qualified Faculty and Staff. High-Tech Lab is also facilitating the patient of CMA Hospital and other hospitals on a panel of CMA Hospital for reliable diagnostic and reporting purposes in Histopathology, Cytopathology, and FNAC Procedures.

Anatomage Lab

At Superior University Azra Naheed Medical College (ANMC) Anatomy department have an anatomage table which is an emerging technology. Anatomage had developed as one of the innovative approaches for medical education teaching and learning tool for better understanding of basic sciences. The aim of this study was to explore the practicality of the virtual body dissection table and inclusive gratification among medical students in the teaching and learning of gross human anatomy. As a major basic medical science, learning human anatomy could be expressively wedged with real visual anatomy. Therefore, the use of virtual body dissection technology gives the impression to have an auspicious role in upcoming educational training programs. In the state-of-the-art Anatomage hall provide 3D Dissection with 8 LED screens installed to provide better understanding and for maximum accommodation of students.
